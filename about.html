About Me!

As an artist, I have always been fascinated by images and how the world is represented through the eyes of others. This has lead me to enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive user designs beyond the visual and ergonomic aspect of an application.

Just as many artists have echoed the idea “No man wants to be an artist. He is driven to it.”
It is never been my decision to go into the arts. I seem to have been born with that already decided. all that was left for me was to acknowledge that and find the best ways to engage my creativity.

Narurally I have always been talented in art & photography. I am currently a part time Artist. With full play role as a FullStack & Android developer

I have made things for as long as I remember. And I have tried almost most of the arts, failing at some, succeeding at others. Some did not hold my interest. From all I learned, from each I took something that I still employ in my current work.

I have always stived to have a positive attitude towards life as I firmly believe, "Choosing your mental peace over everything is the only way to live."

I'm an ambitious developer with a responsible approach to tasks I undertake as I always pushing myself to grow by setting firm goals for myself. Someone who demonstrates an unwavering resolve to do whatever I believe in, as long as it will help me grow and bring positive impact to the society.

Anytime the fear of something bothers me, I choose to silence the world around me and turn the pain to art

When I'm not coding, doing art or photography, you'll find me-:

I believe we sometimes fail to realize that life isn't just a series of goals to be achieved. But rather a journey to be celebrated along the way. We get caught up in commitments and before we realize we are asking ourselves, "What did I really spend the most formidable years of my life doing?" We fail to explore the world not just geographically. But also in its depth by failing to connect with those around us and listening to what our gut really tells us.

All this because we are afraid. We fear what we cannot predict. Ask yourself, "What makes you uncomfortable?" "What I'm I going to do about it?" USE FEAR TO DRIVE YOU FORWARD. The good things in life are always on the other side of fear.

Life is a cycle and a string of mysteries and surprises. We just have to live, enjoy, survive, learn and collect experiences from it and leave kindness, laughter, tears and good memories behind as we stive to make the best of ourselves…. THAT’S HOW I SEE LIFE.
